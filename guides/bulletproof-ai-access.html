Proxy / VPN Considerations

Do we need a proxy? No. And here's why:

  • Residential IP: We're on a home MacBook with a normal residential IP. This is the BEST setup ‚Äî looks like a normal user.
  • VPN/Proxy risk: Actually INCREASES ban risk. Shared IPs, datacenter IPs, and VPNs are flagged by Anthropic's abuse detection.
  • VPS caveat: If we ever move to a VPS, use a reputable provider (Hetzner, DigitalOcean) with a dedicated IP. Cheap VPS providers share IPs and get flagged.
‚ö†Ô∏è Do NOT use proxies or VPNs for AI API access. They make you look suspicious, not safer.
