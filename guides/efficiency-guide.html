What's "Context Window" and Why It Matters

Every conversation has a context window â€” the amount of text Forge can "see" at once (200K tokens â‰ˆ ~150,000 words). As conversations get longer, context fills up. When it hits the limit, OpenClaw runs compaction â€” it summarises the conversation to free up space.

Compaction = token burn. Every compaction costs tokens because Forge has to re-read and summarise everything. Long conversations = more compactions = more cost.

Efficiency tip: For new topics, start a fresh conversation rather than continuing a 3-hour-old one. Fresh context = less waste.

  --task "Run product research per SOP. Research 3-5 health/wellness products. Save to pipeline." \
