Key Principles

üìñ Real Stories > Made-Up Stories

  • Pull from real customer experiences ‚Äî they resonate more
  • Expand on real stories, combine elements from multiple if needed
  • Trust person = friend, coworker, family ‚Äî someone believable

ü§ù Invalidate Competitors Fairly

  • Don't trash other products ‚Äî acknowledge them
  • "We tried bamboo sheets... nice but didn't work for us"
  • Validates the reader's experience of trying other things first

‚úÇÔ∏è Brief = Map. Stick to It.

  • Without a brief, copy gets WAY too long
  • You'll have tons of research ‚Äî doesn't all go in the advertorial
  • Each section has a job. If a paragraph doesn't serve that job, cut it.

ü§ñ Why Manual Research Before AI

  • AI produces overwhelming amounts of content
  • Manual research first = you know what you're looking for in AI output
  • Brief + manual research = filter for AI's massive output

Stretch Mark Stick

Headline: "Is this new mess-free stick the stretch mark solution busy moms have been searching for?"

Lead: Story of trying oils, creams, lotions ‚Äî all greasy, messy, ruined clothes. About to give up...

Trust person: Friend/sister discovered the stick format

Invalidate: "Tried Bio-Oil... nice smell but my sheets were ruined." "Tried cocoa butter... 10 minutes every morning, still greasy."

Reveal: The stick format ‚Äî 30 seconds, no mess, done.

USP: It's not you, it's the format.
