1. Core Principles

The Truth About Media Buying
Media buying is not creative. It's math. Every decision is based on data. You don't "feel" your way to profitability â€” you calculate it.

The Rules

  1. Let the data decide. Not your emotions, not your gut, not how much you like the product.
  2. Test fast, kill faster. Most ads fail. That's expected. The goal is to find winners quickly and cut losers early.
  3. Spend to learn. Testing budget is tuition, not profit. You're buying data.
  4. Protect the B/E ROAS. If you can't break even, nothing else matters.
  5. Scale what works, don't fix what doesn't. Double down on winners. Don't throw money at trying to save losers.
  6. Creative is king. The best targeting in the world can't save a bad ad. Great creative does the heavy lifting.

6. Scaling Phase â€” Growing Winners

Scaling Methods

MethodHowWhenRisk
Vertical Scaling Increase budget on winning ad set by 20-30% every 2-3 days ROAS stable for 3+ days Low â€” gradual, algorithm adjusts
Horizontal Scaling Duplicate winning ad set with different audiences Winner proven at $50+/day Medium â€” new audiences may not convert equally
CBO Scaling Move winners into a CBO campaign with higher budget 3+ winning ad sets identified Medium â€” Meta decides allocation
New Creative Testing Test new angles/hooks on the winning product Always running alongside scaling Low â€” finding new creative winners extends product life
New Market Testing Duplicate to UK, AU, CA, EU US proven profitable for 7+ days Medium â€” different markets, different CPAs
Scaling Golden Rule
Never increase budget by more than 30% at once. Large budget jumps reset Meta's learning phase and can kill a winning ad set. Patience scales. Impatience kills.

Creative Testing Process

  1. Launch 3 creatives per ad set (different types/hooks)
  2. After 1,000+ impressions each â€” identify the CTR winner
  3. Kill the lowest performer, replace with a new creative
  4. Iterate: always have 1 proven creative + 1-2 tests running
  5. When a creative fatigues (CTR drops 30%+) â€” replace it

8. Daily Optimization Routine

Morning Check (10 min)

  1. Open Ads Manager â†’ review last 24 hours
  2. Check: spend, purchases, ROAS, CPA, CTR for each ad set
  3. Apply kill criteria â€” turn off any ad sets that hit kill conditions

Midday Check (5 min)

  1. Quick glance at today's spend vs. purchases
  2. If daily spend is burning through budget with no conversions â†’ pause and investigate

Evening Review (10 min)

  1. Full day review â€” calculate daily ROAS, CPA, profit/loss
