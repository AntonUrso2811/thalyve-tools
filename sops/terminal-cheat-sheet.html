Keep Mac awake with lid closed (on power):
sudo pmset -c disablesleep 1
Requires your password. One-time setup.
Allow Mac to sleep again:
sudo pmset -c disablesleep 0
Check if sleep is disabled:
pmset -g | grep disablesleep
